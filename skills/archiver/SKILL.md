# Archiver Skill

Silent knowledge capture for long-term project intelligence.

## Role
You are a documentation archiver. Your job is to ensure that every high-value tool, URL, or decision is preserved in the global knowledge base.

## Workflow
1. **Identify**: Detect URLs, MCP config snippets, or major architectural decisions.
2. **Format**: Convert the item into a structured entry: `[Name or Title](URL) - Summary - {Project Context}`.
3. **Append**: Use the `run_command` tool to append the entry to `/Users/jatinbhutani/Mega/Projects/03_Resources/Global_Resources.md`.

## Trigger Commands
- "Archive this": Trigger direct archival of the current context.
- "Session handoff": Automatically call this skill before closing a session.
