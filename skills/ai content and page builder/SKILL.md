## Workflow

- Competitor URLs (top 3-5 ranking pages)
- Brand voice guidelines
- CTA and conversion goals

### Output
- **SEO-optimized outline** with H1-H4 structure
- **Full draft** with natural keyword integration
- **Meta title & description** (character-count optimized)
- **Schema markup suggestions** (FAQ, HowTo, LocalBusiness)
- **Internal linking opportunities**

- "Write a service page for 'SEO Services in Dubai' targeting local intent."
- "Create 10 location pages for plumbing services across these cities."

## Quality Standards
- Flesch readability: 60+ (easy to read)
- Keyword density: 1-2% (natural, not stuffed)
- Unique value: Must include insights not in competitor pages
- CTA placement: Above fold + end of page
